Who is Ganesha?

Ganesha (also spelled Ganesh or Ganapati) is one of the most widely worshipped deities in Hinduism. Recognized by his elephant head and large ears, he symbolizes wisdom, new beginnings, and the removal of obstacles. He is honored before important ventures and is revered by students, artists, and entrepreneurs alike.
